Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick meal, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes

  • Preparation : 5 minutes

  • Cooking : 5 minutes

Ingredients
  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetebles, coocked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1.   Beat the eggs:  In a bowl, beat the eggs with salt and pepper utill they are well mixed. Yo can add a tablespoon of water or milkfor a fluffer texture.

  2.   Heat the pan:  place a non sticky drying fan over the heat and add butter and oil.

  3.   Cook the omelette:  Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure that the eggs evenly coat the surface.

  4.   Add fillings (optional):  When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your choosen fillings over the half of the omelette.

  5.   Fold and serve:  As the omelette starts to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold itover the fillings, then slide into a plate.
